How to Play Number Snake

Use the arrow keys to move the snake

Eat the numbered circles to grow your snake

Each numbered circle will contain a number from 1 to 5

This number represents how many points the circle is worth,
and how much your snake will grow

Orange spikes are stationary, and will appear at game start

Red spikes move slowly horizontally and vertically,
and begin appearing at a game score of 25 and above

Pink spikes move fast in all directions,
and begin appearing at a game score of 65 and above
